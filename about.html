About TakeHomeCalc

TakeHomeCalc.net is a free salary and take home pay calculator covering the UK, United States, Canada, and Australia. We built it because understanding your actual net pay — after all taxes and deductions — shouldn't require an accounting degree.

Enter your salary, choose your country, and instantly see exactly how much ends up in your bank account — broken down clearly and simply.

Countries Covered

🇬🇧 United Kingdom

Income tax, National Insurance, pension contributions, student loan deductions. 2024/25 tax year rates.

🇺🇸 United States

Federal income tax, Social Security (6.2%), and Medicare (1.45%). 2025 tax brackets.

🇨🇦 Canada

Federal income tax, provincial tax estimate, CPP contributions, and EI premiums. 2025 rates.

🇦🇺 Australia

Income tax and Medicare levy. 2024/25 financial year. Low income tax offset included.

How We Calculate

All calculations use the latest published tax rates and thresholds for each country. For the UK, we apply the 2024/25 income tax bands, National Insurance Class 1 rates, and pension auto-enrolment rules. For the US, we apply the 2025 federal tax brackets plus FICA deductions. Results are estimates — individual circumstances vary.

Important disclaimer: TakeHomeCalc.net provides estimates for informational purposes only. Tax rules are complex and individual circumstances vary. Always consult a qualified tax professional or accountant for advice specific to your situation.
